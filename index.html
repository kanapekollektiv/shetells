Let me tell you, baby, how it all started
The ocean looked at herself and one day thought: I could be more. So she built a bubble.
Soft. Ridiculous, barely there. Just a little membrane holding a pocket of herself away from herself.

And then she went everywhere

The bubble is YOU, Sweetheart. Still carrying the ocean inside. Your blood, your tears, the fluid in your spine. Ancient seawater in a soft container, walking around, acting all unbothered.

You did not leave the ocean. You wore her.

When you stand on the beach, and something in your chest recognises the salt in the air...
That is not nostalgia, baby..
That is ocean checking on her investment.
